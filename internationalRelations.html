What is International Relations?

Global Politics, Peace, and Power

International Relations (IR) is the study of how nations interact, cooperate, and conflict. It covers diplomacy, foreign policy, global organizations, international law, and security.

Edu Navia helps you explore the vast world of IR — from theory and policy to real-world diplomacy and peacebuilding careers.
