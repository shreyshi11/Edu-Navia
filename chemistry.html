What is Chemistry?

The Central Science

Chemistry is the scientific study of substances, their properties, structures, and transformations. It bridges physics, biology, and engineering — and drives advances in health, energy, environment, and materials.

At Edu Navia, we help you explore diverse pathways in Chemistry — from academic research to applied sciences and global industries.
