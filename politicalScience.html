What is Political Science?

Power, Politics & Public Affairs

Political Science is the systematic study of governments, political processes, and public policies. It explores the impact of ideologies, systems, and governance on people and the world.

At Edu Navia, we guide you through a deep academic journey in Political Science — preparing you for roles in law, civil services, research, diplomacy, and public administration.
