What is Biotechnology?

The Fusion of Biology & Technology

Biotechnology combines biology with technology to create solutions for health, agriculture, environment, and industry. From gene editing to biofuels, it's a frontier of innovation.

At Edu Navia, we guide you through various pathways in Biotechnology — from lab techniques to genetic engineering and industrial applications.
